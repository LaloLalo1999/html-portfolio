Lalo's favorite movies

Top 3 movies according to Lalo

Climax - Gaspar Noe

Great movie about a group of dancers having a none-concensual acid trip gone terribly wrong in the middle of the winter in rural france.

Samsara - Ron Fricke

Beautiful documentary about the human condition and the world we live in.

The Holy Mountain - Alejandro Jodorowsky

Great movie about a group of people trying to reach the top of the holy mountain.
